--- layout: default title: LogoLyze - The Graphical Analyzer ---
LogoLyze is a software to display an analyze graphs of all kind that are available in an OLAP database. LogoLyze is optimized for maximum flexiblity, regarding analyzation capabilities as well as the possibility to use the software with differently structured databases.

Graph OLAP has been handled in theory by several scientists in a paper. According to our research, there has not been an easy to use, production ready software to visualize this data.

The software has been developed in a practical course at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology by Timo Paulssen, Florian Larysch, Andreas Fried, Uwe L. Korn and me. The project was motivated by the research project Logotakt.
